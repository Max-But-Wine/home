    How it works

    1. Choose tier: Cheese Club, Cheese + Wine Club, or Cellar Select.
    2. Set preferences: red / white / mixed, cheese style, allergens.
    3. Add delivery details and subscribe via SA recurring gateway.
    4. Receive monthly chilled delivery with tasting + pairing notes.
    5. Manage skip, pause, upgrade, gifting, and address updates anytime.

    Payments (SA recurring options)

    Recommended providers: PayFast Subscriptions, Peach Payments recurring, or Paystack recurring APIs.

    Launch path to reduce risk

    Launch Cheese Club first (non-alcohol fulfilment), then add licensed wine partner add-ons before taking in-house liquor operations.

    FAQ

    Can I skip or pause?

    Yes, before billing date in Account. Pause updates pause_until.

    Can I gift membership?

    Yes, gift one month from the member portal actions.

    What about allergens?

    How is age verification handled?

    Wine options require 18+ attestation at checkout and ID check on delivery.

      Legal & compliance

      Liquor (if wine is included)

      • Use valid provincial off-consumption liquor licensing.
      • Ensure online/telephone fulfilment rules align with licence conditions.
      • Record invoices, deliver to invoiced address, retain records.
      • Enforce 18+ verification and delivery ID checks.

      Food handling (perishable cheese)

      • Maintain Certificate of Acceptability requirements for food handling.
      • Apply hygiene controls to storage, pack stations, and transport chain.
      • Follow ingredient + date/batch labelling obligations.

      Terms & Conditions

      Monthly subscriptions auto-renew. Skip/pause/cancel changes apply from the next bill cycle before cut-off dates. Cold-chain delivery attempts require recipient availability.

      Privacy (POPIA)

      We process contact, address, preference, and order data to fulfill memberships. Members may request updates or deletion where lawful.

      Returns

      Perishable products are non-returnable unless damaged, unsafe, or incorrect on delivery. Report issues within 24 hours.
